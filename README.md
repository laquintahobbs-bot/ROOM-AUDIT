# La Quinta Hobbs - Room Audit Scanner

A lightweight, mobile-optimized web application designed for housekeeping supervisors and front desk staff to execute the **"Tight Five" Pre-Check-In Room Audit**. This tool uses native device cameras to scan room QR codes, instantly bringing up an interactive checklist to verify cleanliness and room readiness before keys are handed to guests.

---

## 📋 The "Tight Five" System
To drive immediate improvements in our Cleanliness and Guestroom satisfaction scores, every audited room must pass five zero-tolerance parameters:

1. **Hair Check (Zero-Tolerance):** Tub, shower tiles, floor corners, and vanity countertops must be 100% hair-free.
2. **Linen & Bed Quality:** Verify bedding underneath the comforter is crisp, taut, and completely unstained; fresh towels staged.
3. **Surface & Corner Wipe:** Ensure high-visibility tabletops, desk surfaces, and nightstands are free of dust, smudges, or rings.
4. **Tech & Climate Check:** Confirm the TV remote is present and functional; verify the split-unit AC/Heater powers up cleanly.
5. **Essentials & Clothes Hangers:** Ensure basic bathroom amenities are stocked and a minimum of 4 hangers are in the closet.

---

## 🚀 How to Use on Shift

### 1. Scanning a Room
* Open your smartphone's native camera app and point it at the QR code plaque outside or inside the guestroom.
* Tap the link to launch the dashboard.
* Tap **Initialize Camera** to use the built-in scanner, or use the manual entry box at the bottom to type in the room number directly.

### 2. Conducting the Audit
* Go through the room and check off each item on the **Active Audit** tab. 
* If the room is perfect, tap **✅ PASS & CLEAR**.
* If you find a maintenance issue or a cleanliness defect, type the details into the **Maintenance Remarks** box and tap **❌ FAIL ROOM**.

### 3. Reviewing History
* Tap the **Logs** tab to see all rooms checked during the current shift. 
* *Note: Data is saved locally to your device's browser cache for quick reference on shift.*
